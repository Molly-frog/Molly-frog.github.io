Nico's Pasta With Prosciutto, Onions, Peas and Pancetta

Why Is It Important To Our Family?

We make this dish all the time, and the whole family just really enjoys it. We never have left overs when we make it because everyone is have seconds or even thirds. When my sister went to Dublin she made it and said it was just a part of her and it felt like she was giving her flatmates a little piece of herself. We're not Italians so it's not our culutre but it sure does mean a lot to us.

Here We Go!

Serving: 4-6

Ingredients

  • Salt and Pepper, to taste
  • 1lb Fettuccine
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 3 Tbsp Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion, Finely Chopped
  • 1 Small Shallot, Finely Chopped
  • 1/2 Clove Garlic, Thinly Sliced
  • 2 Cups Coarsely Diced Pancetta
  • 4 Slices Prosciutto, Coarsely Chopped
  • 1/4 Cups Of Noodle Water??(Water from cooking the noodles)
  • 1 Cup Peas
