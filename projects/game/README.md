# Wanted: The Straw Hat

## Setting & Story

Set in the One Piece world, you are a weary travler wandering the seas. One day a big storm hits and you try to stay afloat, however ultimately you end up unconscious and wake up to find that you are on the Straw Hats Ship. You sit in your cabin and see the Captian himself sleeping by your bed. He wakes up and asks you to find his straw hat, you decide to help him! You really want to pay him back for saving you! You must find his hat!!!

## Global Variables

The most important variables are havehat which is a boolean that tracks if you have found the hat or not. The value only ever becomes true when you look in the mirror and realize you are wearing the hat, so then you can back and return it to Luffy. Otherwise, if you go into a room and talk to a character they will tell you they dont know where the hat is, but if the value is true then they will tell you that you are wearing the hat.
